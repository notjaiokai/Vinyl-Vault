About Us

In a world where everything is going digital and physical music is becoming harder to find, The Vinyl Vault stands as a sanctuary for true music lovers. This is where music really lives. We celebrate the warmth of vinyl, the nostalgia of CDs, the charm of cassettes, and the artistry of album covers you can actually hold in your hands.


We're more than just a music store—we're passionate advocates for preserving physical media in an age of endless streaming. Whether you're rediscovering timeless classics or building your collection from the ground up, we believe music deserves to be experienced, not just heard.

Custom Vinyl Pressing

Have a favorite album or playlist? Send us your tracks, and we'll press them onto vinyl just for you—because your music should be as unique as your taste.

Curated Collections

Explore our carefully selected vinyl records, CDs, cassettes, and other physical media, spanning every genre and era. Every piece is chosen with collectors in mind.

A Community of Collectors

Join a community of fellow enthusiasts who understand that music isn't disposable—it's meant to be cherished, displayed, and passed down.
